About Proctor PTA

The Proctor Elementary PTA (Parent Teacher Association)

The Proctor Elementary PTA is a very active organization of amazing parents and teachers. We are affiliated with the state and national PTA organizations (See National PTA website for more information).

Membership is open to all parents, teachers, and family and community members. We have an Executive Board that is elected annually in March each year and committee chairs for each of our enrichment programs and fundraisers. Our Executive Board and Committee Chairs design and implement PTA programs and activities with help from family volunteers and teacher input school wide. Our principal and a teacher representative are active participants in the PTA Executive Board Meetings.
