Fundraising Events

Proctor PTA holds two major annual fundraising events, the Walkathon in Fall, and the Online Auction in Spring. The school uses funds raised to pay for library and computer lab support, educational aides, targeted reading and math help for students who need support and teacher supplies.

PTA Supports

Proctor PTA offers support to teachers through office and classroom work, to the community through outreach and to our students through enrichment in art and music (FAME) and values and problem solving skills (PEP). We also host several events through the year to bring families together as a school community including, Back-to-School Potluck, Fall Carnival, Holiday Ceramics Shop, Skate Night, and Book Fairs.
