Association Meetings

First Tuesday Monthly @ 6:30pm

The PTA strives to provide community enrichment to parents through general PTA Association Meetings and other special events. The Association meets the first Tuesday of every month at 6:30 in Multi-purpose room (we do not meet in November) to share a brief and general review of monthly budget items (detailed budget reports are shared electronically with Association Members upon request), news of upcoming programs and events, as well as an update from the principal. Some PTA meetings feature a special guest speaker and cover topics such as the district/school budget and district news, childrenâ€™s health and safety, or more in-depth information on academics. Alternatively, some meetings are designed to keep the business short to allow parents a better chance to connect with other parents as well as teachers and staff or to recognize our most valuable asset, our volunteers.
